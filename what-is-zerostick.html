Our Mission

Performance You Can Feel

A ZeroStick is a custom gaming controller built around performance, comfort, and personalization. Starting with genuine first-party Sony controllers, we enhance them with modifications designed to help players react faster, play longer, and compete at their best.

From casual gamers to competitive players, every ZeroStick is built with the same commitment to quality and precision.
