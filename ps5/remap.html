Remap Instructions

How to remap your ZeroStick rear buttons — no app or software required.

Before you start: Make sure your controller is on and connected. Remapping does not require a USB cable or any software — it's done entirely on the controller itself.

How to Remap

Step-by-Step Guide

1

Enter Remap Mode

Hold down the remap button (located on the back of the controller) for 3 seconds until the light bar flashes. You are now in remap mode.

2

Select the Rear Button to Remap

Press the rear button (Left or Right) that you want to assign a new input to. The light bar will flash to confirm your selection.

3

Press the Button You Want to Map

Now press the face button, trigger, bumper, or stick click that you want the rear button to mirror. For example, press ✕ to map jump to the rear button.

4

Confirm the Mapping

The light bar will flash again to confirm. You can now remap the second rear button by repeating steps 2–3, or exit remap mode.

5

Exit Remap Mode

Hold the remap button again for 3 seconds until the light bar returns to solid. Your new mappings are saved and active.

Resetting to Default

To clear all rear button mappings and return to default, hold the remap button for 8 seconds. All custom mappings will be erased.
