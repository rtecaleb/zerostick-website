What Are Edge Modules?

Swappable Stick Modules

The DualSense Edge features swappable thumbstick modules — small cartridges that contain the stick mechanism and can be replaced without tools. This makes them ideal for upgrading to drift-resistant or hall-effect mechanisms.

ZeroStick offers upgraded modules that fit directly into your existing DualSense Edge, giving you a performance boost without a full controller build.

Installation

How to Swap Your Modules

1️⃣

Push & Release

Press the small release button on the back of the Edge and slide the module out. No tools needed.

2️⃣

Slide In New Module

Align the ZeroStick module with the slot and slide it in until it clicks. That's it.

3️⃣

Calibrate (if needed)

Hall-effect modules may require a quick calibration via the Edge's built-in stick calibration tool in PS5 settings.
