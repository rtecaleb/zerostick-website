What Is a ZeroStick?

Smart Triggers

Smart Triggers replace the standard analog trigger mechanism with a mouse-click style switch. This gives you a much shorter, crisper actuation point — your shots register the instant you pull, with no travel lag or analog variance.

  • ✓ Instant actuation — no travel delay
  • ✓ Consistent every press
  • ✓ Compatible with all PS5 games

Smart Bumpers

Same mouse-click technology applied to your bumpers. Crisp, tactile, and lightning-fast. Never miss a perfect reload or ability activation again.

Drift-Resistant Sticks

Upgraded thumbstick mechanisms engineered to resist the wear that causes drift. Our hall-effect option uses magnetic sensing with zero physical contact — virtually no drift, ever.
