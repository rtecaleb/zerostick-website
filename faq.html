Controllers & Modifications

We use genuine first-party Sony DualSense (PS5) controllers as our base. We never use third-party shells or knockoff hardware.
Yes. Since we start with genuine DualSense controllers, they work on PC via USB or Bluetooth just like a standard DualSense would, with full compatibility across most games and launchers.
Smart Triggers and Bumpers replace the standard analog inputs with mouse-click style switches. This gives you a much shorter, crisper actuation point — faster inputs with no travel lag.
Drift-resistant thumbstick mechanisms use upgraded components that resist the wear-and-tear that causes stick drift in standard controllers. Our hall-effect option uses magnetic sensing with zero physical contact, meaning virtually no drift over time.
Rear button remapping is done directly on the controller without any app or software. See our step-by-step Remap Instructions page for a full guide.
