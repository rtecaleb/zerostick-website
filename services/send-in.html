How It Works

Send-In Process

1

Submit a Request

Fill out the form on this page with your details, controller type, and what service you need. We'll confirm your request by email.

2

Ship Your Controller

Pack your controller securely and ship it to our address (provided in your confirmation email). We recommend using a tracked shipping service.

3

We Get to Work

Once received, we'll inspect your controller and perform the requested service. Turnaround is typically 5–7 business days.

4

We Ship It Back

Your controller is returned to you fully tested, with a tracking number and summary of the work completed.
