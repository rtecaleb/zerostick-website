Warranty Policy & Repairs

Your ZeroStick is built to last. Here's what's covered and how to get support if something goes wrong.

Coverage

What's Covered

✓ Covered

  • • Defects in our modifications or workmanship
  • • Faulty rear buttons or triggers from installation
  • • Stick drift caused by our upgraded mechanisms
  • • Loose or unresponsive buttons due to assembly
  • • Cosmetic defects present at time of delivery

✗ Not Covered

  • • Physical damage (drops, water, impact)
  • • Damage from third-party modifications
  • • Normal wear and tear
  • • Cosmetic wear from use
  • • Controllers purchased second-hand

Warranty Period

All ZeroStick controllers come with a [X]-month limited warranty from the date of delivery, covering defects in our modifications and workmanship.

Warranty is non-transferable and applies to the original purchaser only. Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) is required for all warranty claims.

Making a Claim

How to Request a Repair

1

Contact Support

Reach out via our Support page with your order number and a description of the issue. Include photos or video if possible.

2

Receive RMA Authorisation

We'll review your claim and issue an RMA (Return Merchandise Authorisation) number if approved. Do not send your controller before receiving an RMA number.

3

Ship Your Controller

Send your controller to us using a tracked shipping method. Include your RMA number inside the package. Return shipping is covered by ZeroStick for valid warranty claims.

4

Repair & Return

We'll inspect, repair, and ship your controller back within 5–7 business days of receiving it.
