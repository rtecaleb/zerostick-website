Returns

Our return policy — what's eligible, how to start a return, and what to expect.

Because our controllers are built to order, custom-built and Quick Pick controllers are non-refundable unless there is a defect in workmanship covered under warranty. Pre-built in-stock controllers may be returned within [X] days of delivery in original, unused condition.

Eligibility

What Can Be Returned

✓ Eligible for Return

  • • Pre-built in-stock controllers (within [X] days)
  • • Accessories and parts (unopened, unused)
  • • Items with a defect at time of delivery

✗ Not Eligible

  • • Custom-built controllers
  • • Quick Pick controllers
  • • Used or opened accessories
  • • Items returned after the return window
  • • Physically damaged items

Process

How to Start a Return

1

Contact Us Within the Return Window

Email us or use the Support page with your order number and reason for return. Returns must be initiated within [X] days of delivery.

2

Receive Return Authorisation

We'll review your request and send return instructions. Do not ship anything back until you've received authorisation.

3

Ship the Item Back

Pack the item securely in its original packaging and ship it back using a tracked service. Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective.

4

Refund Issued

Once we receive and inspect the item, your refund will be processed within 3–5 business days to your original payment method.
