Phob Drop-In Boards

The PhobGCC is a drop-in board replacement for GameCube controllers that eliminates stick drift through hall-effect sensing, adds notch filtering, and enables precise calibration — all without modifying the shell.

We offer pre-built controllers with Phob boards already installed, DIY kits, and drop-in boards for players who want to install it themselves.
